About This App

This application is a simple tool to generate random passwords and check the strength of passwords you enter. It's mainly for learning and experimentation, showing what makes a password stronger or weaker.

The password strength checker looks at factors like length, character variety, and randomness to give a rough estimate of security. This is not a professional assessment and may not reflect real-world safety.

Important Notice

This is an incomplete project and should not be used for any critical accounts or sensitive information. Avoid entering real passwords, including:

  • Banking or financial account passwords
  • Email account passwords
  • Social media login credentials
  • Work or personal authentication passwords
  • Any password connected to important or private services
