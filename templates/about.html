🔐 Importance of Secure Passwords

Think about how many things in your life are protected by a password—your email, social media, games, school accounts, even banking apps.

Now imagine someone guessing that password in seconds because it’s something simple like 123456 or password.

A secure password acts like a strong lock on your digital door. The longer and more complex it is—mixing uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols—the harder it becomes for someone to break in.

By choosing strong and unique passwords, you’re protecting your identity, your data, and your control over your online life.
